Omurice

Description

A classic Japanese breakfast dish.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • rice
  • ketchup
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Add desired amount of rice to cooker and begin cooking.
  2. When rice is about done, make an omlette.
  3. Place omlette on finished rice, and sprinkle on some salt and pepper.
  4. Add however much ketchup you like and serve.
