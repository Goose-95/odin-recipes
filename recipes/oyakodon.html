Oyakodon

Description

A delicious and easy to prepare rice bowl featuring chicken and eggs.

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • rice
  • greed onion
  • spices
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Add desired amount of rice to rice cooker and start it.
  2. Chop green onion and chichen thighs.
  3. Cook chicken thighs over medium high heat with whatever spices you prefer.
  4. While chicken cooks, beat as many eggs as you like.
  5. After rice is finished, quickly pour the eggs in and mix together.
  6. Top the eggy rice with cooked chicken and green onion and serve.
